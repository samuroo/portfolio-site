I am a current Undergraduate student at the University of California Santa Cruz studying Robotics Engineering with an Electrical Engineering minor. I am passionate about engineering tools facilitate meaningful human interaction and contribute positively to society.


The work showcased on this site includes projects from my involvement in Slugbotics Club, where I lead a team of undergraduate researchers in a 3DoF robotic arm, as well as my contributions to the Robotic Control Lab, and personal projects developed. These experiences have shaped my ability to learn, communcate with others and passion for robotics.


Beyond my academic pursuits, I enjoy slacklining, cooking, and playing hacky sack—activities that keep me grounded as I continue exploring the world of robotics.
