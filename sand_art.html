  In this project I was invited by Fruitcake, a University of California Santa Cruz (UCSC) art club, to create a piece of art for a one day gallery. My love for guitar and visual art gave me inpiration for my submission:

Constuction

"Wave Finder"

Components included a six inch 8 OHM speaker taken from a small speaker cabniet found in a dumpster, quarter inch aryrlic, a mono guitar output jack, 16th inch alumnium plate and some loose hardware found in my house. Of course, sand was found at the beach and sifted for a finer powder.


At the showing, I would pluck a single guitar note. amp with extra output in back.
