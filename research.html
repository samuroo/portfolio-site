As a member of the Robotics and Control Lab, my research focuses on advancing robotics with an emphasis on real-world applications in agriculture. One of our key projects is enhancing the capabilities of the Amiga tractor, an autonomous electric tractor developed by Farm_NG. Designed to support organic farming, the Amiga enables low-till weeding practices that reduce the reliance on harmful pesticides and herbicides, promoting sustainable farming methods.
